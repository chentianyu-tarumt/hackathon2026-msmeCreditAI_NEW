## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account
- Open the app
- Click the **"Register"** tab
- Enter a **username** and **password** (minimum 6 characters)
- Click **"Register"**
- Switch to the **"Login"** tab and sign in

---

## 📋 How to Run a Credit Assessment

Once logged in, you will be on the **"New Assessment"** page.

### Step 1 — Fill in Business Profile
| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Business Name | Your business name (e.g. ABC Enterprise) |
| Industry | Select the closest industry category |
| Years in Operation | How long the business has been running |
| Number of Employees | Total staff including yourself |
| Avg Monthly Revenue (RM) | Average income per month |
| Avg Monthly Expenses (RM) | Average costs per month |
| Has Business Bank Account | Whether you have a dedicated business bank account |
| Loan Amount Requested (RM) | How much financing you are applying for |

### Step 2 — Fill in Additional Signals
| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Online/Digital Presence | Do you sell or advertise online? |
| Accepts Digital Payments | Do you accept e-wallet or online payments? |
| Supplier Relationships | How long have you worked with your suppliers? |
| Customer Base | Are most of your customers repeat or new? |

### Step 3 — Submit
- Click **"Analyse Creditworthiness Now!"**
- Wait a few seconds for the AI to analyse your profile

---

## 📊 Understanding Your Results

After analysis, you will see:

### Credit Score (300 – 850)
| Score Range | Tier | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 700 – 850 | 🟢 High | Strong creditworthiness, good loan eligibility |
| 500 – 699 | 🟡 Medium | Moderate creditworthiness, may qualify with conditions |
| 300 – 499 | 🔴 Low | Needs improvement before applying for financing |

### What Else You Get
- **AI Summary** — Plain-language explanation of your score
- **Recommended Loan Range** — Suggested financing amount based on your profile
- **Strengths** — What your business is doing well
- **Risk Factors** — Areas of concern for lenders
- **How to Improve Your Score** — Actionable tips to boost your creditworthiness

## 📂 My History & Analytics

Click **"My History & Analytics"** in the navigation to view:

- **Total assessments** you have run
- **Average credit score** across all assessments
- **High tier count** — how many businesses scored High
- **Average loan requested**
- **Charts** — breakdown by tier and industry
- **Full records table** of all past assessments
- **Export to CSV** — download your records as a spreadsheet
- **Delete a record** — remove any assessment from history

> 🔒 Your history is private — only you can see your own records.

## 💡 Tips for a Better Score

1. **Register your business bank account** — it signals financial stability
2. **Build an online presence** — even a Facebook page helps
3. **Accept digital payments** — e-wallets like Touch 'n Go or GrabPay are positive signals
4. **Maintain long-term supplier relationships** — consistency builds trust
5. **Keep expenses in check** — a healthy profit margin improves your score
6. **Add context in the notes field** — explain seasonal patterns or recent growth

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. Your data is stored securely in Firebase and is only accessible with your account login.

**Q: Does this replace a real bank credit check?**
A: No. This tool is designed to help you understand your creditworthiness and improve it before approaching a lender. It is not an official financial assessment.

**Q: Can I run multiple assessments?**
A: Yes! You can assess different businesses or track your improvement over time by running multiple assessments.

**Q: What if my score is Low?**
A: Don't worry — check the "How to Improve Your Score" section for specific steps you can take to raise your score.
